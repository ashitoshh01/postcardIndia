About Us

Who We Are

Postcard is one of the oldest medium to communicate. However, the nature of postcard has evolved with time. We, here in Postcard & Epic Marketing India think, postcard was never dead, it has only evolved.

What does a modern postcard serve? It gives instant results and works with all channels. It is efficient, flexible and cost effective. And we resonate with each values of modern day Postcard.

Our Philosophy

We don't just tell your story; we show your consumers how we blend perfectly in their story. Engage and demonstrate them on how your brand can add value to their day to day lives.

Our Approach

Developing campaigns by Thinking like a Consumer, entering their Mindset and building Creative Insightful Campaigns that will create a long lasting impression.

Our Aim

Insightful Campaigns are more relatable and need no introduction hence it results into higher brand recall value. If your campaign did not spark strong emotions then the communication has left no impact in their minds.
